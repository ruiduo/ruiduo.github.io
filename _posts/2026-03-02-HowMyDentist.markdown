9 am. Get up, peel off layers of mouth tape, and spittoon out the 2 cups of Pepsicola product I left in my oral cavity overnight. I stumble to the bathroom and flop my toothbrush around in horizontal strokes with the 5 second rule. Perfect.

9:30 am. Pour myself a bowl of nuts and bolts for breakfast. Stir 'em around and go to town with my titanium chopsticks. 

10 am. Go outside and get scared at the power lines. I've never seen those before, such spindly wires. I'd never put something so ductile in my mouth. Never. 

11 am. Call my orthodontist and send her a gift basket. Thank her for the good work she does for my teeth and patiently schedule my next appointment six months in advance. DM my dentist's hot dental hygienist for a rush appointment tomorrow. Deal with it. 

12 pm. For lunch, I packed myself an alternating course of burning hot soup and ice cream. I like to surprise my mouth.

1 pm. Snack time! The green ferrules on the Ticonderogas are my favorite. Nom nom nom. Score! I brought my keys from home today too. 

3 pm. Bored and seeking stimulation. I see the sharp edge of my all metal safe. I wonder how my precious, will-never-grow-back-again teeth would feel on this. I bite down. My dental insurance will cover this [it won't].

5 pm. I go to pick up my kid from daycare. I've been running a White Albert experiment on little Johnny except I fire a pistol every time he sees a dentist. Dunno why. I just don't see them as people.

7 pm. Dinner tonight is mozzarella sticks, thick cylindrical baby carrots, steak cut fries, and sausage links - really anything that's shaped like my dentist's perfect gloved fingers. That way tomorrow I'll salivate and make a sticky mess all over my dentist's sterile gloves.

11 pm. I throw out my toothbrush that I haven't replaced in a year. It's time to get replacements for the whole decade at my dentist's office. I hope they have pink.
